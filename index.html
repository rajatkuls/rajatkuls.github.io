I'm a graduate student working as a research assistant under Prof. Jaime Carbonell at the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University.

My interests lie in building systems that can accumulate information from different input modalities and leverage it to make intelligent decisions and adapt to new inputs.
